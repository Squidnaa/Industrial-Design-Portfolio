About me

Sydney Montalto

I am deeply passionate about exploring how we interact with the world around us, focusing on human ergonomics, robotics, and software development. My interests lie at the intersection of design and technology, where I strive to create solutions that are both user-friendly and innovative. With a strong attention to detail and a love for problem-solving, I am dedicated to understanding and improving the way people engage with products and systems. My background in industrial design enhances my skills in UI and software development, allowing me to develop and implement human-centered solutions.
