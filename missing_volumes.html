Missing Volumes Furniture Set

To create a furniture set of three that includes 1 light fixture and 1 physically prototyped object. I created a chair, a table, and hanging light fixture and prototyped the table. The philosophy of my design was to use planar and linear materials in order to leave out volumes and have the eye fill in the rest. I used metal tubing which I bent and welded and poplar sheets that I laminated and bent into shape.
