Jelly Robot for Alternate Futures Prompt

A collaborative project between an industrial designer (me), an interior designer, and a digital designer to create a story based off of an alternate future. As the industrial designer, I had to create an accessory for our main character, Alex. Alex is a scientist who conducts a particle research experiment that goes terribly wrong, opening a portal to an alternate Earth and pulling him in with it. Alex meets a Jelly Robot that helps guide him through this new world. The project involved sketching, prototyping, modeling, and rendering. I also created the portals for the story and movie posters.
