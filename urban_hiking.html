Urban Hiking for Sustainable Future- Arc’tyrex brand study

Tasked with picking a brand, I chose Arc’tyrex, to look into their sustainability initiatives and create a line of products for an existing initiative or new one. With brand researching I chose to create a line of products based off of their microplastic studies which concludes that microplastics from rubber soles leach toxic substances into the ground and limit plant growth. I designed a set of urban hiking poles and shoes that encourage outdoor activities in urban environments and are made with natural rubbers to not affect the environment.
