East Coast Free-Ride Skis

The east coast skiing environment presents unique challenges for those who venture off-piste. Skis that can perform well in deep powder and variable conditions such as; icy crusts, slush, packed powder, obstacles, and other mixed terrain are required. I sought to design and develop a set of off-piste skis specifically tailored towards east coast skiing, focusing on durability, playfulness, lightweight construction, and stability. With this I created a ski brand, called Solstice Skis, that has three skis in it, early season, peak season, and spring. I full prototyped the spring ski, the Spring Break.
